## Initial Validation Plan

### Target Respondents

* Undergraduate students who need academic support.
* Students who perform well academically and may become tutors.

### Questions to Ask

1. Have you ever needed help in a subject but did not know whom to ask?
2. How do you currently find academic help?
3. Would you use a platform to find peer tutors?
4. Would you be interested in becoming a tutor?
5. What information would you like to see about tutors?
6. What features would make the platform useful?

### Evidence to Collect

* Number of students who need tutors.
* Number of students willing to become tutors.
* Problems with current alternatives.
* Interest level in the proposed platform.
* Most requested features.
